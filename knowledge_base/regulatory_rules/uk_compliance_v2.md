# UK Regulatory Rules - V2.0.0
# Active From: 2026-01-01

- **UBO Threshold**: 25% ownership or control.
- **Sanctions Check**: Must check HMT, OFAC, and EU lists.
- **Address Verification**: Required for all directors.
- **Nature of Business**: Must be verified against SIC codes.
- **High Risk Jurisdictions**: Enhanced Due Diligence (EDD) required for countries on the FATF grey/black lists.
