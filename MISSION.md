# AI KYB Agent Mission & Context

We are building a truly autonomous AI KYB Agent — an intelligent “investigator + reasoning system” that completely replaces today’s rule-based “pipes + rules” KYB platforms. 

The product is a multi-agent AI system capable of receiving minimal input (company name, registration number, or uploaded documents) and independently planning, executing, and reasoning through complex investigations to produce a full, accurate, and regulator-ready KYB profile. 

## Key Capabilities:
- **Dynamic Data Gathering:** Independently gathers data from global registries, commercial databases, sanctions lists, financial documents, and unstructured web sources.
- **Ownership Resolution:** Resolves multi-layered ownership structures and Ultimate Beneficial Owners (UBOs).
- **Document Intelligence:** Extracts and corroborates information from PDFs and scanned files.
- **Advanced Reasoning:** Handles ambiguities and edge cases through chain-of-thought reasoning, self-critique, and recursive tool use.
- **Explainable Audit Trails:** Generates detailed explainable audit trails with confidence scores and evidence provenance.
- **Privacy & Sovereignty:** Supports privacy-preserving verifiable credentials with explicit consent management.
- **Near-Zero Intervention:** Delivers near-zero manual intervention for the vast majority of cases.

Unlike existing solutions that auto-fill only clean data and escalate everything complex to humans, this agent acts like a skilled compliance analyst — adapting its investigation depth, balancing speed and accuracy, continuously monitoring for changes, and providing transparent, high-confidence decisions that dramatically improve straight-through processing rates while meeting stringent regulatory requirements. 

The end result is a 10x better KYB experience: faster, more accurate, more complete, and far less dependent on human review.
